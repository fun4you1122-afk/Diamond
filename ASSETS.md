### Tips for best results
- **Photos:** JPG, landscape or 4:5 portrait, ≥1600px on the long edge. I'll compress if huge.
- **Hero video:** MP4 (H.264), muted, no audio needed. Keep it under ~15 MB if possible for fast load.
- **Send as-is** — don't worry about exact names or sizes; I'll rename, crop-fit and slot each one in, then push.
